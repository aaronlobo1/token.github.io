Token Features

The FARM Tokens are ERC 20 Tokens. Some features of the FARM token that make it stand out from the rest and give you peace of mind.

Flexibility & Easy to Use

The token is designed prioritizing simplicity and ease of use to accelerate platform adoption

Continuous Liquidity

Token holders can use tokens to directly buy products or services from our partner companies at a discount.

Supports sustainability

We care about wildlife, environment and its well being. A part of the funds are allocated to create awareness about sustainability.

Think different

At FARM group we believe that entrepreneurs are the ultimate agents of positive, transformational change across every aspect of our society and economy.
