How it Works

OVERVIEW

How it works

Great food companies can come from anywhere. With the help of our private Token community we intend to identify,evaluate and partner with 10 - 15 promising food companies a year. As those companies scale and move into new markets they increase the value of the FARM ecosystem, the FARM Tokens and subsequently provide more venues for token liquidation. Below is a simplified flow diagram on how we intend to create value for our token backers and for companies that we partner with.

For example, let us assume that we partnered with amazing boutique Tea companies located in Morocco,India,Sri Lanka, China and Japan with the intention of launching and distributing their products in the USA. The supply chain logistics, licenses and regulations to distribute the tea in the USA would be the same for all the tea's irrespective of their origin. Infact the same network can also be used to launch coffee as well!!

The Flow Process

PROCESS

Farm Token Foundation

FARM Tokens

As our newly partnered food company grows, the value of the FARM ecosystem increases and the companies provide more venues to liquidate the token.
